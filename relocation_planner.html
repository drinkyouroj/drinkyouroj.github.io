CRITICAL: Vantage Bridge

The I-90 Vantage Bridge (WA) has an **absolute 9-foot width limit** through 2028. Many medium-duty trucks with towing mirrors or wide-body enclosed trailers exceed this.

Action Required:

Measure the widest point of your rig (including mirrors) before departure. If > 108 inches, you cannot cross this bridge.
